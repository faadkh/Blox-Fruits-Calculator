Combo Optimization Metrics

Key performance indicators for evaluating and improving combat combo effectiveness:

Damage per second (DPS) measures combo efficiency. Total damage indicates combo power. Execution time affects combo viability in fast-paced combat. Success rate reflects combo reliability under pressure.

Balance these metrics based on your playstyle and competitive goals. High-risk, high-reward combos suit aggressive players, while consistent, moderate-damage combos work for defensive strategies.
