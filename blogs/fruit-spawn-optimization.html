The spawn system operates on fixed intervals that vary by fruit rarity, server population, and specific game conditions. Understanding these variables is crucial for developing effective collection strategies that consistently yield high-value fruits.

Spawn Timing Classifications

Common Fruits (45-minute intervals)

Common fruits spawn frequently and provide excellent opportunities for new players to build their initial inventory. While less valuable individually, their consistent availability makes them perfect for trading and early-game progression.

Rare Fruits (60-minute intervals)

Rare fruits offer balanced value and spawn frequency, making them ideal targets for intermediate players. These fruits often provide significant combat advantages while remaining reasonably accessible through dedicated farming.

Legendary Fruits (2-hour intervals)

Legendary fruits require patience and strategic timing. Their extended spawn intervals demand careful planning and often benefit from coordinated farming efforts with friends or crew members.

Mythical Fruits (3+ hour intervals)

The most valuable fruits in the game, mythical fruits require exceptional dedication and timing precision. Professional fruit farmers often organize their entire gameplay schedule around these high-value spawns.
