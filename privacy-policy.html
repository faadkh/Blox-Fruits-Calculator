Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns:

  • Browser type and version

Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when:

  • Using our contact form

Our calculators and tools process your input data locally in your browser. This information is not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save or share results. Calculator inputs and results are temporary and automatically cleared when you navigate away from the page.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Website Functionality: To provide and maintain our gaming tools and calculators
  • User Experience: To improve website performance and user interface design
  • Analytics: To understand how our tools are used and identify popular features
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Security: To protect against spam, abuse, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure hosting with reputable service providers
  • Regular backups with encrypted storage

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, and functionality:

We use CDNs to deliver website content efficiently. These services may collect technical information about your connection and location for performance optimization.

Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses cookies and local storage for:

  • Remembering your preferences and settings

Children's Privacy

Our website is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information promptly.
